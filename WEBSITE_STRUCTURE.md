## 📄 **VIEWS (What Shows Below the Header)**

### **1. Welcome (Landing Page) — Default**

- **4 feature cards:** Structured Dua, Personal Expression, Problem-Based Guidance, Save & Organize  
- **How It Works:** 3 steps (Select Categories, Express Your Dua, Receive Structured Dua)

---

### **2. Structure Your Dua Page** (Opened by "Structure Your Dua" button)

- **Instruction:** "Select categories and express your dua in your own words"
- **Categories:** 8 checkboxes — Rizq, Health, Marriage, Exams, Anxiety, Forgiveness, Guidance, Protection
- **Label:** "Express your dua or problem in your own language:"
- **Record Voice** button (red) — Start/stop recording; transcribed text appends to textarea
- **Clear Recording** (when there is transcribed text)
- **Textarea** — Type or paste; placeholder explains typing or using Record Voice
- **Actions:**
  - **Structure Your Dua** (purple, main) — Generates full structured dua
  - **Reset** (gray) — Clears categories, input, and generated result

**After generating:** Same page shows the full generated dua (10 sections + Problem-Based Guidance) and Save option.

---

### **3. Generated Dua Block** (On Structure Your Dua page, after clicking "Structure Your Dua")

- **Heading:** "Your Dua" with save/bookmark icon
- **10 mandatory sections:**  
  1. Praise Allah  
  2. Salāh upon Prophet Muhammad ﷺ  
  3. Ism-ul-A'ẓam Dua  
  4. Calling Allah by Relevant Names  
  5. Humility & Weakness  
  6. **Your Personal Dua** (generated from input + categories; focused/hybrid logic)  
  7. Clear Request  
  8. Tawakkul (Trust in Allah)  
  9. Salāh upon Prophet Muhammad ﷺ Again  
  10. Āmīn  
- **Problem-Based Guidance:** Relevant Dhikr, Relevant Dua, Qurʾānic Motivation (prophetic story)

> **Planned next-generation dua structure (not fully implemented yet)**  
> For future versions, the dua generator will follow a refined prophetic-style flow built from fixed, authenticated blocks and a curated Qurʾānic dua database. The target structure is:
>
> 1. **Praise Allah** – Rich praise using verified wording and Names of Allah (includes Ism-ul-Aʿẓam dua).  
> 2. **Declare Tawḥīd** – Short, fixed block affirming Allah’s oneness (no AI variation).  
> 3. **Confess Weakness** – Admit sins, limits, and neediness, with wording chosen from a safe set based on the user’s emotional state (e.g. guilt, fear).  
> 4. **Ask Forgiveness** – Insert Qur’anic duʿās for maghfirah from a curated database (Arabic + transliteration + translation + surah/ayah).  
> 5. **Ask for Worldly Needs** – Duʿās matched to the user’s categories (rizq, health, protection, family, etc.), always using verified Arabic for Qur’anic text.  
> 6. **Ask for Hereafter Success** – Always include hereafter-focused Qur’anic duʿās (e.g. 2:201 and others).  
> 7. **Duʿā for Others / Ummah** – General good for parents, family, and the Ummah; some Qur’anic and some clearly labeled non‑Qur’anic supplications.  
> 8. **End with Ṣalawāt** – Fixed, authenticated ṣalawāt on Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.
>
> AI will only help with: (a) detecting user intent and emotional theme from text + selected categories, and (b) adding English explanatory glue around fixed/Qur’anic content. Arabic for Qur’anic duʿās will **never** be generated by AI and will only come from a verified dataset.

### **5. Best Times — Times & Situations When Dua Is More Likely to Be Accepted** (Header: “Best Times”)

- **Page title:** “Times & Situations When Dua Is More Likely to Be Accepted”
- **Section 1 — Sahih:** 6 items (last third of night, sujood, Friday hour, Laylatul Qadr, dua of oppressed, dua for others in absence), each with Why, Proof, references, Authenticity
- **Section 2 — Hasan:** 7 items (between Adhan and Iqamah, iftar, after obligatory prayers, Day of ‘Arafah, traveling, when raining, parents’ dua for children)
- **Section 3 — Qur’anic Prophetic:** 2 items (Dua of Yunus, after Istighfar)
- **CTA at bottom:** “Structure Your Dua →”

### **6. Why Dua Not Answered** (From More ▾)

- **Intro:** "Why Dua Is Not Answered" — message that delay ≠ rejection; self-reflection not self-blame

- **Summary:** Scholar’s summary + **"Structure Your Dua →"** button back to generator
